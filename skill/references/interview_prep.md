## Question Sources

- Resume bullets: ask for architecture, tradeoffs, constraints, metrics, and lessons learned.
- JD requirements: ask system design and technology questions that map to the role.
- Gaps from match report: ask how transferable experience maps to missing skills.
- Leadership claims: ask STAR-style questions about mentoring, conflict, delivery, and decision-making.

Keep questions specific to the user's actual resume and the target JD.
