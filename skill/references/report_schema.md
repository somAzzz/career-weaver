# Match Report

Write match reports to `output/{person}/jobs/{job}/deliverables/match_report.md`.

## Structure

```markdown
# Resume-JD Match Report

## Overall Score: XX/100

## Executive Summary
Brief assessment of profile vs JD fit.

## Key Matching Skills
- Skill or evidence that aligns with the JD

## Critical Missing Keywords
- Important JD keyword not supported by the profile

## Experience Gap Analysis
| JD Requirement | Profile Strength | Gap Level |
|----------------|------------------|-----------|
| Requirement | Evidence or gap | High/Medium/Low |

## Strategic Recommendations
1. **Priority 1**: Actionable advice.
2. **Priority 2**: Actionable advice.
3. **Priority 3**: Actionable advice.

## Suggested Resume Focus
- Summary angle
- Experience to emphasize
- Skills to emphasize or de-emphasize
```

Score honestly. Missing critical requirements belong in gaps, not in the resume.
