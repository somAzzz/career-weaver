Senior Platform Engineer - Observability

Company: SignalForge
Location: Remote - US

SignalForge provides observability tooling for engineering organizations running
microservices on Kubernetes. We are hiring a Senior Platform Engineer to improve
our internal telemetry platform and help product teams understand service health.

Responsibilities

- Build and operate observability infrastructure for logs, metrics, traces, and SLOs
- Create reusable dashboards, alerting standards, and service health scorecards
- Partner with product engineering teams to instrument Go, Python, and Node.js services
- Improve Kubernetes platform reliability and reduce alert fatigue
- Participate in on-call rotation and lead post-incident reviews
- Build internal tools that help teams understand deployment, latency, and error-rate trends

Required qualifications

- 5+ years of backend or platform engineering experience
- Production Kubernetes experience
- Strong experience with Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry, or comparable tooling
- Strong programming ability in Go or Python
- Experience designing incident response, SLO, or alerting practices
- Comfort writing clear technical documentation for other engineers

Preferred qualifications

- Experience with Kafka or high-volume event pipelines
- Experience with Terraform or Helm
- Experience reducing cloud infrastructure cost
- Experience building developer-facing tools
- Experience speaking or writing about reliability practices
