Engineering Manager - Platform

Company: HarborCloud
Location: Remote - US

HarborCloud is hiring an Engineering Manager for our platform organization. This
person will manage a team of 8 engineers responsible for infrastructure,
deployments, reliability, and internal developer tooling. The role includes
people management, hiring, performance reviews, roadmap planning, and cross-team
coordination.

Responsibilities

- Manage and grow a team of backend and platform engineers
- Own performance management, career development, hiring, and compensation input
- Set quarterly roadmap priorities with product and engineering leadership
- Ensure platform reliability, delivery predictability, and healthy on-call practices
- Coach engineers through technical decisions without becoming the bottleneck
- Track team metrics and communicate status to senior leadership

Required qualifications

- 2+ years of formal engineering management experience
- Experience hiring, performance management, and career coaching
- Strong technical background in backend, infrastructure, or platform engineering
- Experience managing roadmaps for developer productivity or reliability teams
- Strong written and verbal communication skills

Preferred qualifications

- Experience managing managers or tech leads
- Experience with Kubernetes, AWS, CI/CD, and observability
- Experience scaling engineering teams through organizational change
- Experience with budget planning or vendor management
