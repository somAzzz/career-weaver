Technical Lead - Developer Platform

Company: OrbitWorks
Location: Seattle, WA; San Francisco, CA; or Remote - US

Overview

OrbitWorks is expanding its internal developer platform team. We need a Technical
Lead who can remain hands-on while setting architecture direction for build,
deploy, observability, and service reliability systems used by 150+ engineers.

What you will do

- Lead technical direction for CI/CD, service templates, infrastructure automation,
  and observability standards
- Drive architecture reviews for platform changes that affect many product teams
- Partner with engineering managers and product leaders to prioritize platform roadmap work
- Mentor senior and mid-level engineers through design reviews, pairing, and feedback
- Improve operational excellence through SLOs, incident review practices, and alert hygiene
- Reduce developer friction by identifying slow, flaky, or confusing parts of the release process
- Communicate technical tradeoffs in planning meetings and written design documents

Required qualifications

- 7+ years of software engineering experience
- 2+ years providing technical leadership across a team or major workstream
- Strong backend engineering experience in Go, Python, Java, or similar languages
- Production experience with Kubernetes, Docker, and cloud infrastructure
- Experience with CI/CD systems such as GitHub Actions, Jenkins, Buildkite, or GitLab CI
- Strong understanding of distributed systems, reliability, and incident response
- Evidence of mentoring engineers and improving engineering practices

Preferred qualifications

- Experience scaling platform adoption across multiple product teams
- Experience with Terraform, Helm, Prometheus, Grafana, or OpenTelemetry
- Background in developer tools, infrastructure platforms, or B2B SaaS
- Experience writing technical blog posts, internal RFCs, or architecture decision records
- People management experience is not required
