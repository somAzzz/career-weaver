Senior Backend Engineer - Payments Platform

Company: Northstar Commerce
Location: San Francisco, CA or Remote - US
Employment type: Full time

About the role

Northstar Commerce builds payment, subscription, and billing infrastructure for
mid-market SaaS companies. We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to help
design reliable APIs, event-driven workflows, and internal tools used by product
teams and customer success. This role is hands-on and works closely with product,
security, support, and infrastructure teams.

Responsibilities

- Design, build, and maintain backend services for payment orchestration, invoices,
  subscription lifecycle events, and customer account workflows
- Build REST and event-driven APIs that support web, mobile, and partner integrations
- Improve reliability, observability, and incident response for customer-facing services
- Profile and optimize high-throughput services backed by PostgreSQL, Redis, and Kafka
- Write automated tests and support continuous delivery practices
- Partner with security and compliance teams on secure coding, audit logs, access control,
  and dependency management
- Mentor mid-level engineers through code reviews, design feedback, and technical planning

Required qualifications

- 5+ years of backend software engineering experience
- Strong production experience with Python, Go, Java, or similar languages
- Experience designing REST APIs and distributed systems
- Experience with AWS or comparable cloud infrastructure
- Strong SQL and relational database experience, preferably PostgreSQL
- Practical experience with Docker, CI/CD, observability, and on-call operations
- Ability to communicate tradeoffs clearly to technical and non-technical stakeholders

Preferred qualifications

- Experience with Kafka or another event streaming platform
- Experience with Kubernetes and infrastructure as code
- Familiarity with payments, billing, fintech, compliance, or audit logging
- Experience reducing cloud cost or improving deployment speed
- Experience mentoring engineers without necessarily being a people manager
